Stephen Colbert

Americas Dad

Life and Career

  • 1964: Born
  • 1974: Tragedy strikes, and Stephen loses his father and two brothers to an airplane accident
  • 1982: Begins school at Hampden Sydney College to study philosophy
  • 1984: Transfers to Northwestern University to study Theater
  • 1986: Gets a job at second city Chicago answering phones and selling souvenirs
  • 1995: Moves to New York to work on sketch show Exit 57 which ran for one season on Comedy Central
  • 1996: Begins work on the Dana Carvey Show as a writer and performer
  • 1997: Hired for The Daily Show
  • 1999: Develops Strangers with Candy with Amy Sedaris and Paul Dinello for Comedy Central
  • 2005: Leaves The Daily Show to host his own show, The Colbert Report
  • 2015: Leaves The Colbert Report to succeed David Letterman as the host of The Late Show.
